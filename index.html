PARKOUR LABS Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2, 2026

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Welcome to PARKOUR LABS. Your privacy is of the utmost importance to us at Pdpartid@games.

This privacy policy explains how we handle information in our virtual reality application available on the Meta Quest Store.

The summary is very simple: We do not collect, store, use, or share any personal data from our users. Your gaming experience is entirely yours and stays on your device.

What Personal Data We Do Not Collect

To be absolutely clear, when we say we do not collect personal data, we mean that we do not request or have access to any type of information that could identify you. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Name, username, or email address
  • Device data such as IP address or unique device identifiers
  • Game statistics, achievements, or gameplay actions
  • Location data
  • Audio or video recordings
  • Information from your Meta account or Horizon profile

Everything that happens in PARKOUR LABS stays on your Meta Quest headset.

Data Processing

Since we do not collect any data, there is nothing to process, handle, or use. The application operates autonomously on your device without sending any personal information to our servers or to those of third parties.

Third Parties

We do not integrate analytics tools, advertising, or third-party services into our application that would collect data on our behalf.

To download and run the game, you use Meta’s services. Meta may collect data according to its own policies. For example, cloud saves are managed by Meta and are subject to their privacy policy.

We recommend reviewing Meta’s Privacy Policy to understand how they manage your data.

Data Deletion Requests

In accordance with Meta’s guidelines, users must have a clear way to request the deletion of their data. Since we do not collect or store any personal data, there is no data for us to delete.

Game information is stored locally on your device and can be removed by uninstalling the application.

Children’s Privacy

We respect the privacy of children. Our application does not knowingly collect any personal information from any user, including children.

Use of Meta Quest devices is subject to Meta’s Terms, which specify a minimum age for users.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

Although we do not anticipate changes due to our no-data-collection policy, if future updates introduce features that require data collection, this policy will be updated clearly and prominently.

You will be informed of any changes before continuing to use the application.
